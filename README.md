# Retail Sales & Customer Segmentation Analytics

## 📌 Business Impact
- Identified customer segments contributing majority revenue using RFM analysis on 50,000+ transactions
- Detected ~15% lower repeat purchase behavior in low-frequency customer segment
- Identified high return-rate product categories causing revenue leakage (25–30% range)
- Enabled targeted retention strategy for high-risk customers

---

## 📊 Problem Statement
Analyze customer transaction behavior to identify high-value customers, at-risk segments, and product-level revenue leakage.

---

## 📦 Dataset
- 50,000+ retail transactions
- Customer-level purchase history
- Product category and return/profit data

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Champions segment contributed majority of revenue
- At-risk customers showed significant drop in purchase frequency
- Certain product categories showed consistently high return rates impacting profitability
- Customer value distribution was highly skewed (few customers = majority revenue)

---

## 📌 Outcome
- Improved customer segmentation strategy
- Identified revenue leakage sources
- Supported retention-focused business decisions
